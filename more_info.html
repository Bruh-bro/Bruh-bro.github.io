What's the future of the school's snack shop?

The future of the school snack shop would be handed to the future of every year 10. The snack shop prices and the way they sell the snacks is entirely depending on the future year 10s. The snack shop can change entirely or be the same as it was like last year.

The teachers and students would be able to experience different kinds of businesses as the year who's organising the snack shop change every year. The teachers and students will have to adapt and learn how the new snack shop is runned.

Will the snacks change every year?

There is a possibility that the future snack shop organisers could add new snacks or get rid of old snacks. One thing for sure the price could change or stay exactly the same. The future snack shop organisers will have to learn what makes the students and teachers happy to keep wanting to come and buy snacks from them.

The year 10's who currently owns the snack shop can change the set up for the better or to see how the customers react to it. The snack shop could change daily or barely change, but for sure the snack shop will only change to see if the customers react positvely or negatively to the change. If they react negatively of course they will bring back the old set up, if not it could stay the same way for about a month or two.
